What do we do?

Game Developers is a low commitment, chill club for anyone interested in exploring the art
of making games, or even just learning the basics of a few programming languages. We also have
access to a computer lab so personal devices are optional (atleast with the 2D section). You can
also just come in and play games, or do your homework while we go over slides that introduce
developing.

Why Should You Consider Joining?

For freshman and sophmores, this club is a great oportunity to get a head start on Computer
Science by learing the format of a language based on Python. First semester will consist of 2D
development of the game engine "Godot". Second semester is planned to be 3D development on
Unity with C#. By the end of the year, you will have knowledge on two engines, two dimensions,
and two languages. All lesson slides will be posted on this website after the meeting, but it is
recommended that you come in and try it yourself.

Game Jams

At the end of every semester, we will have our Game Jam. Here, we show off the games we
made in the time allocated and play them together. There will be an itch.io page for you
to post your submissions. The link will be in the "Resources" page on this website when
submitions are open. The person who creates the best game to be judged by the leaders, will
get a prize!
