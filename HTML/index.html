What do we do?

Game Developers is a low commitment, chill club for anyone interested in exploring the art of making games, or even just learning the basics of a few programming languages. We also have access to a computer lab so personal devices are optional (atleast with the 2D section). You can also just come in and play games, or do your homework while we go over slides that introduce developing. First semester will consist of 2D development of the game engine "Godot". Second semester is planned to be 3D development on Unity with C#.

Why Should You Consider Joining?

  • This club is a great oportunity to get a head start on Computer Science
  • You will gain knowledge on two engines and dimensions
  • You will be familiar with two programming languages

Game Jams

At the end of every semester, we will have our Game Jam. Here, we show off the games we made in the time allocated and play them together. There will be an itch.io page for you to post your submissions. The link will be in the "Resources" page on this website when submitions open. The person who creates the best game to be judged by the leaders, will recieve a prize!
